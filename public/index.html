Core Features

ShopStream Feed

An infinite scroll of short-form videos showcasing products, demos, and business stories. Every video is shoppable with a "Buy Now" button.

Direct Checkout

A seamless, in-app checkout system. Customers purchase directly from your video content without ever leaving the LocalLoop app.

Verified LBO Profiles

Build trust with robust business profiles, including verification, bio, product catalogs, and your physical location on a map.

VocalForLocal Recommendations

Gain credibility with professional endorsements from other verified business owners. Customers can also leave product reviews.

The Town Hall (B2B)

A dedicated networking space to find local partners, discuss supply chain needs, and share industry tips with other business owners.

Local Discovery

Users can filter the ShopStream to see content from businesses in their immediate area, encouraging true local shopping and discovery.
