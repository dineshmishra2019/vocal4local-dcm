Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my business verified?

To become a Verified LBO, navigate to your profile settings and select the "Request Verification" option. You will be asked to submit your business registration documents. Our team will review them within 3-5 business days.

What is the transaction fee on sales?

VocalForLocal charges a small transaction fee (between .01-.05%) on every successful sale made through the Direct Checkout feature. This fee helps us maintain the platform. If you don't make a sale, you don't pay anything.

How do VocalForLocal Recommendations work?

Verified LBOs can give and receive professional endorsements to each other. This builds a network of trust. Customers can also leave reviews on products they've purchased. You can request a recommendation from a partner business directly from their profile page.

How do I track my order as a customer?

You can view your order history and tracking information in your user profile under the "My Orders" tab. You will also receive email notifications when your order is shipped.
